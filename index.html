About Me

Obaid Khan

I am a passionate mathematics educator with over 6+ years of teaching experience, guiding students from middle school to college level. My journey in education began with a deep fascination for the beauty and logic of mathematics, and I strive to share this passion with every student I teach.

My teaching philosophy focuses on making mathematics accessible, engaging, and relevant. I believe that every student has the potential to excel when concepts are presented clearly and connected to real-world applications.
